General comment: Resist stripping

Description:

This recipe can be followed for strip resists such as: AZ1512, LOR and AZ9260.

Steps

SVC-14 strip: Deep the sample in SVC-14 and sonicate at the appropriate power for 3'.
IPA cleaning: Deep the sample in IPA and sonicate at the appropriate power for 3'.
Drying: N2 gun
O2 plasma: 5' Tepla Gigabatch high power
